About Me


Originally from the coastal city of Corpus Christi, I decided to pursue an education at the University of Texas at Austin after graduating high school at the top 10% of my class. I enrolled at UT with a focus in Advertisement, but eventually switched my focus to Radio-Television-Film. After receiving my Bachelor of Science in Radio-Television-Film in 2018, I enrolled in UT's Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp in 2020.

My higher education led me to discover an interest in telecommunications and computer science, by learning its history, as well as the modern impact on society and our daily lives. I leveraged this newfound insight by entering Austin's ever expanding tech industry as a Technical Support Representative for BigCommerce, a leading E-Commerce company.

Currently, I spend my career troubleshooting storefronts from small business to enterprise level. I love to assist my clients and colleagues to understand and achieve the fullest potential of our software. I spend my time helping analyzing error reports, assisting with front-end design, database management, as well as installing countless integrations and finding the best business solutions for every angle.
